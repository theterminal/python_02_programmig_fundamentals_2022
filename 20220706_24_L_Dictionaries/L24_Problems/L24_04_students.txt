04 - Students - judge url: https://judge.softuni.org/Contests/Practice/Index/1736#3


You will be receiving names of students, their ID,
and a course of programming they have taken in the format:
"{name}:{ID}:{course}".

On the last line, you will receive a name of a course in snake case lowercase letters.

You should print only the information of the students who have taken the corresponding course in the format:
"{name} - {ID}"
on separate lines.

Note: each student's ID will always be unique


_____________ Test Data ______________


Input 1:
-------
Peter:123:programming basics
John:5622:fundamentals
Maya:89:fundamentals
Lilly:633:fundamentals
fundamentals


Output 1:
--------
John - 5622
Maya - 89
Lilly - 633


---------------------------------------


Input 2:
-------
Alex:6:programming basics
Maria:7:programming basics
Kaloyan:9:advanced
Todor:10:fundamentals
programming_basics


Output 2:
--------
Alex - 6
Maria - 7
